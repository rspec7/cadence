About this plan
Running in standalone (home screen) mode
Your setup
40-year-old male · WPW post-ablation · Weight loss + mobility focus · Runs 3x/week (M/W/F, ~2 miles). Supplements a carb-conscious diet.
WPW & heart rate
Keep perceived effort at 5–6/10. Target heart rate zone: 100–130 bpm during exercises. If you hit 150+ and feel it, stop and walk slowly for 60 seconds. No explosive movements in this plan. Consult your cardiologist before starting if you haven't already.
Sets guidance
Phase 1–3 (Weeks 1–6): 1 set per exercise. The goal is habit and form.

Phase 4 (Weeks 7–8): 2 sets per exercise. Rest 45–60 sec between sets.

Phase 5+: 2–3 sets, selectively increasing to harder tier variations.
Diet notes
Keep a small carb source (banana, rice, oats) within 30 min post-run for muscle recovery. Aim for 0.7–0.8g protein per pound of bodyweight. The running + this routine will do the fat-loss work without starvation.
